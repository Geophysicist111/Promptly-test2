About Life Assistant

Our Story

Life Assistant was created to help you navigate those tricky social situations with ease and creativity. Whether you need a clever excuse, a heartfelt compliment, or a thoughtful gift idea, we've got you covered.

How It Works

Our generators use a combination of creativity and context to provide you with unique, personalized content for every situation. The more you use Life Assistant, the more it learns your preferences!
